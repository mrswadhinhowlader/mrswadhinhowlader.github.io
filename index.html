About My Work

I build smart, end-to-end business automation systems using n8n. These systems are designed to reduce manual work, respond faster to customers, and help businesses scale without hiring extra people.

Most businesses lose valuable time on repetitive tasks—replying to messages, capturing leads, updating spreadsheets, sending follow-ups, notifying admins, and maintaining basic operations. Individually these tasks feel small, but together they slow growth and create operational chaos.

My work focuses on replacing these manual processes with reliable automation systems that run silently in the background while your business looks active, responsive, and professional on the front end.

I specialize in Messenger Automation and WhatsApp Business Automation, where customer messages are handled automatically in a structured and human-friendly way. Leads are captured, common questions are answered instantly, and important conversations are routed to admins in real time with full context.

These systems can auto-reply based on customer intent, collect user information step-by-step, trigger follow-ups, update Google Sheets or CRM dashboards, and alert team members instantly—without losing control of the conversation.

Another key area of my work is building LinkedIn realtime viral post generator systems. These automation workflows help creators, founders, and business owners generate high-quality post ideas, scroll-stopping hooks, and structured content formats in real time—so they can stay consistent and visible without spending hours writing.

Beyond messaging and content systems, I can build all types of AI-powered business automation using n8n—including lead qualification, customer support routing, order handling, reporting dashboards, internal workflow automation, and multi-channel integrations across Messenger, WhatsApp, Email, Google Sheets, and other tools.

Every automation I build is practical, documented, and scalable. The goal is simple: make your business look professional on the front end, while powerful automation runs silently in the background—saving time, reducing errors, and supporting long-term growth.
